A social story is a simple, clear way to teach children or individuals about social situations they find challenging. It uses short sentences and pictures to explain behaviors and expectations. Social stories help people understand how to act appropriately in different settings. They are often used for children with autism or other developmental differences. The goal is to make social skills easier to learn and reduce anxiety. By reading a social story, someone can feel more confident and prepared. Overall, social stories are a helpful tool for promoting understanding and positive interactions.
